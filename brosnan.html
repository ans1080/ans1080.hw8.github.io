Pierce Brosnan



Bond for a new era. The only actor that did not have any movies based on Ian Flemming's books.
He also brought Bond into a more modern era with post Cold War plotlines.
His tenure also saw a more progressive face for the franchise, with him quitting smoking.
And for the first time, his boss, M, was played by a woman, none other than Dame Judy Dench.
