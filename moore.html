Roger Moore



Roger Moore ushered in a new era for the franchise and proved it could survive beyond Connery.
He brought a sense of playfulness and fun to the series that it had never had before.
His movies were much more comedic and outlandish, even finding agent 007 in space on one adventure.
With seven films, he has the most acting credits to his name and he was knighted for his efforts.






The Movies of Roger Moore:


  1. Live and Let Die (1973)

  2. The Man with the Golden Gun (1974)

  3. The Spy Who Loved Me (1977)

  4. Moonraker (1979)

  5. For Your Eyes Only (1981)

  6. Octopussy (1983)

  7. A View to a Kill (1985)
