Timothy Dalton



A darker, more damaged and lethal Bond; Timothy Dalton played Bond by the books.
In fact, he could often be found on set thumbing through the Flemming classics.
His moodier take on the character brought a the franchise back to a more grounded reality.
He never produced a contracted third movie due to a legal dispute between EON productions and MGM.
